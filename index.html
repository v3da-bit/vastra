About Us

At Vastra, every piece tells a story of passion, resilience and dreams. Founded by Ladli Singh, a young entrepreneur with a love for design and a commitment to quality, Vastra has become a bridge between traditional Indian craftsmanship and contemporary fashion.


Ladli’s journey began with a dream to celebrate India’s rich textile heritage by creating contemporary garments that resonate with the current generation. She spent years researching fabrics, designs, and collaborating with artisans across India to create a brand that values ​​art, sustainability, and authenticity. Every piece in our collection reflects Ladli’s commitment to excellence, her respect for traditional craftsmanship, and her vision for a more inclusive and responsible fashion industry.


Our mission is to offer clothing that is as beautiful as it is meaningful – clothing that helps our customers feel confident and connected to their roots. With Vastra, Ladli has created more than just a clothing line, but a platform that supports local artisans, promotes sustainable practices, and celebrates the timeless elegance of Indian fabrics. We invite you to be a part of the Vastra journey, where each piece has a story to tell, crafted with care, inspired by tradition, and designed for the modern age.
